## Differentiators to Emphasize

1. **No MEV** — Batch auctions with uniform clearing price = no front-running, no sandwich attacks
2. **AI Co-Founder** — JARVIS is a full participant, not a chatbot
3. **Fair Launch** — No pre-mine, no VC allocation, no insider advantage
4. **Cooperative Capitalism** — Shapley-distributed rewards, mutualized risk
5. **Omnichain** — LayerZero V2 for cross-chain without bridges

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

- No "to the moon" hype
- No influencer paid shills
- No fake volume or wash trading
- No misleading APY claims
- No promising returns
- Let the mechanism design speak for itself
