Capitalism vs socialism is a false binary.

The real axis isn't left vs right. It's extraction vs cooperation.

Extractive capitalism: privatize gains, socialize losses, rent-seek forever.
Extractive socialism: centralize control, misallocate resources, stagnate.

Both fail the same way. A small group extracts value from everyone else.

Cooperative capitalism: free markets + mutualized risk + structural fairness. Competition on merit, not on who can cheat harder.

That's what we're building. Not a political statement. A mechanism design.

Insurance pools that mutualize tail risk. Priority auctions that let you pay for speed without hurting others. Shapley values that distribute rewards based on actual contribution.

Free markets work. Extraction doesn't. The distinction matters.
