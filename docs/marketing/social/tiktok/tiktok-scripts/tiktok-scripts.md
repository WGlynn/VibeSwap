# VibeSwap TikTok Scripts — 30-60 Second Explainers

## How to Film
- Phone vertical, face-to-camera or screen recording
- Hook in first 2 seconds (pattern interrupt)
- One concept per video. Never two.
- End with a question or call to action
- Caption: 3-5 words that summarize the hook

## Parable 1: "The Two Markets"
**Hook (2s):** "Let me tell you about two markets."

**Body (30s):** "In the first market, the merchant sees you coming. He raises the price before you arrive. The faster runners always get the best deals. If you're slow, you pay more. Everyone accepts it because they've never seen anything else.

In the second market, there's a bell. It rings every 10 seconds. Everyone writes their offer on paper, folds it, and puts it in a box. When the bell rings, all the papers are opened. Everyone pays the same price. The merchant can't see you coming. The fast runners have no advantage."

**Punch (5s):** "The first market is every DEX today. The second market is VibeSwap. Which one do you want to shop in?"

**Caption:** Two markets. Only one is fair.
