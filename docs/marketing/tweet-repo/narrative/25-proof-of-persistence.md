Proof of Stake rewards capital. Have money, make money.

Proof of Work rewards hardware. Have machines, earn coins.

Neither rewards the thing that actually matters.

We need Proof of Persistence.

The person who showed up every day for a year. Who rebuilt after the crash. Who kept shipping when the market said stop.

Capital is replaceable. Hardware is replaceable. The person who won't quit isn't.

That's what fair launch means. Not "everyone starts equal." Everyone starts with exactly what they're willing to put in.
