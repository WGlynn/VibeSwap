Searchers. Builders. Validators.

LAYER 1: SEARCHERS

Bots that scan the mempool for profitable transaction orderings. Sandwich attacks, arbitrage, liquidations.

Thousands of them. Running 24/7. Competing to take your money faster than the next bot.

---

LAYER 2: BUILDERS

Searchers can't insert transactions into blocks alone. They need builders.

Block builders aggregate profitable bundles from searchers and construct optimized blocks. The builder takes a cut for assembling the most extractive block possible.

Your loss is their optimization function.

---

LAYER 3: VALIDATORS

Builders bid for block inclusion. Validators accept the highest bid.

The validator doesn't care what's in the block. They care about the payment. The more value extracted from traders, the higher the bid, the more the validator earns.

Extraction flows upward. Cost flows down to you.
