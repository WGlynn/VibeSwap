Fairness isn't charity. It's not altruism. It's not idealism.

Fairness is the profit-maximizing strategy when you eliminate the ability to cheat.

Think about it. If nobody can front-run, more people trade. More trading means more fees. More fees means more sustainable revenue.

If nobody can extract, trust increases. Trust reduces friction. Reduced friction scales volume.

The only reason unfairness appears profitable is because cheaters externalize their costs onto everyone else. Remove the cheat codes and fairness wins on pure economics.

This isn't philosophy. It's game theory.
